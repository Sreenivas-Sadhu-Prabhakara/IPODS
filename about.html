Founded in 2006, IPODS started as a research and trading company for various engineering disciplines. Over the last decade, we have expanded into a full-service manufacturing facility serving clients globally. Our growth is driven by a simple philosophy: never ship a part we aren't proud of.

From Multi-axis machining center, EDM Wire cutting and Die sinking, to automated CMM inspection, our floor is modern, innovative and efficient.

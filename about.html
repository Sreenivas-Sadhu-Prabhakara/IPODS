Company file · 001 — Origin

Started at a trading desk. Grew into a machine shop.

2006 — founded as a research & trading company for the engineering disciplines.
Today — a full-service precision manufacturing facility serving clients worldwide.

IPODS — Inter-Pacific Outsourced Dimensions Services — was founded in 2006 as a research and trading company serving the engineering disciplines: sourcing, advising and supplying the parts and know-how that technical projects needed to move.

Demand pulled us toward the metal. Client by client, the company expanded from trading into making — building out a full-service precision manufacturing facility that today serves clients globally, from local partners in Laguna's manufacturing corridor to teams overseas who never set foot on the floor.

The standard never moved. Whether the job is a one-off fixture or a production run, the rule at the machines is the same one we started with — and it is not negotiable:

Never ship a part we aren't proud of.

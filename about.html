History

Founded in 2010, IPODS started as a small prototype shop in a garage. Over the last decade, we have expanded into a full-service manufacturing facility serving clients globally. Our growth is driven by a simple philosophy: never ship a part we aren't proud of.

Technology

We invest heavily in the latest CNC technology and metrology equipment. This ensures we stay ahead of the curve, offering capabilities that many other shops cannot match. From 5-axis simultaneous milling to automated CMM inspection, our floor is modern and efficient.
